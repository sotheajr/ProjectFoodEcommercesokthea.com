Spanish Chicken Soup

$5.00

Ingredients

    Directions

  • step 1-Sprinkle chicken with adobo seasoning. In a large stockpot, heat oil over medium heat. Brown chicken on both sides in batches. Remove chicken from pot. Add celery, onion, carrot and sofrito to same pot; cook and stir until onion is tender, 3-4 minutes.
  • Step 2-Return chicken to pot. Add water and bay leaf; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer 30 minutes. Add potatoes. Simmer, uncovered, until potatoes are almost tender, 8-10 minutes.
  • Step 3-Remove chicken and bay leaf; discard bay leaf. Let chicken stand until cool enough to handle. Skim fat from broth. Return broth to a simmer; add noodles. Simmer, uncovered, until noodles are tender, 5-7 minutes.
  • Step 4-Meanwhile

  • 1-1 broiler/fryer chicken (3 to 4 pounds), cut up
  • 2-2 teaspoons adobo seasoning
  • 3-2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4-2 celery ribs, chopped
  • 5-1 medium onion, chopped
  • 6-1 medium carrot, chopped
  • 7-1/4 cup sofrito tomato cooking base
  • 8-2 quarts water
  • 9-1 bay leaf
  • 10-2 medium Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 11-12 ounces fideo noodles or uncooked angel hair pasta, broken into 1-inch pieces
  • 12-1 teaspoon salt
  • 13-1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 14- Fresh cilantro leaves, optional
