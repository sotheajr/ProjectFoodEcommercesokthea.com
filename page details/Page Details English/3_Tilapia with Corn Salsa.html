Tilapia with Corn Salsa

Ingredients

  • 1-4 tilapia fillets (6 ounces each)
  • 2-1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3-1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 4-1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 5-1 can (15 ounces) black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 6-1 can (11 ounces) whole kernel corn, drained
  • 7-1/2 cup Italian salad dressing
  • 8-2 tablespoons chopped green onion
  • 9-2 tablespoons chopped sweet red pepper

    • Directions

  • Drizzle both sides of fillets with oil; sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  • Broil 4-6 in. from the heat until fish flakes easily with a fork, 5-7 minutes. Meanwhile, in a small bowl, combine the remaining ingredients. Serve with the fish.
